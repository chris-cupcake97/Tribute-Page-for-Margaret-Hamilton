Margaret Hamilton

The First Software Engineer

Margaret Hamilton, 1995

Here's a time line of Margaret Hamilton's life:

  • 1936 - Born in Paoli, Indiana
  • 1958 - Graduated from Earlham College
  • 1961 - Started working at MIT on Semi-Automatic Ground Environment (SAGE) project
  • 1965 - Began working on the Apollo space program
  • 1969 - Awarded NASA's Exceptional Space Act Award for her work on the Apollo program
  • 1986 - Founded Hamilton Technologies, Inc.
  • 2003 - Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom from President Barack Obama
  • 2016 - Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom from President Barack Obama

"The software engineering field has not yet learned how to measure complexity. So they manage it in a way that has no intellectual control. That's the tragedy. The tragedy is that they don't know how to manage it in a way that has intellectual control." - Margaret Hamilton
